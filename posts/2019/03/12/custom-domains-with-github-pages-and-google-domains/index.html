Custom Domains With Github Pages And Google Domains

March 12, 2019 , Category: Web Development , Tags: google domains , github , website deployment

Which web address would you rather own: yourname.github.io or yourname.com? The answer is simple: yourname.com. If you are hosting your website on a free service such as Github Pages you'll receive the former, not the later. Fortunately domain names are inexpensive to purchase (if they are available). Depending on the provider the annual cost is usually between $10-20. When comparing the amount you'll spend vs. the benefits you'll receive its easy to justify purchase your own domain.

In this tutorial I will show you how to configure your Github Pages website to be served from a custom domain from Google Domains.

Prequisites

You will need to own a custom domain purchased from Google Domains and have already setup a free static website on Github Pages.

Goals

  • Make your Github Pages website available on yourdomain.com
