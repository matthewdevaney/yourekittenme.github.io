About Me

Hi I'm Matthew, a Chartered Professional Accountant (CPA) based in Winnipeg, Canada. I currently work as a business analyst: searching for answers in a vast ocean of data and using that information to improve the way we do things one process change at a time. Outside of work I am an avid hiker looking for adventure in the great outdoors!

Learning to code started as a practical way for me to automate routine tasks in my job but it quickly developed into a strong interest of mine on it's own merits. I started this website to share what I've learned on my programming journey with the world. Free resources on the web have greatly contributed to my knowledge and now I want to give back to that community and help others to be successful in their projects.
